How It Works

Three simple steps to donate your unused clothes

1

Fill Out the Form

Complete our quick Google form with your contact information and pickup details.

2

Leave your bagged or boxed clothes on your porch at your selected time.

3

We Handle the Rest

Our team picks up your clothes and distributes them through our partner organizations.

About Wear It Forward

Wear It Forward is a student-led nonprofit organization dedicated to making clothing donations simple and accessible while reducing textile waste and helping those in need in our community.

Our mission is to create a sustainable cycle of giving by making the donation process as hassle-free as possible, while ensuring clothes find new homes where they're truly needed.

Our Values

  • Sustainability
  • Community Service
  • Accessibility
